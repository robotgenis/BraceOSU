About

B.R.A.C.E. stands for the Buckeye Robotics Accelerator for Community Enrichement. B.R.A.C.E has the goal of connecting robotics teams with their piers, community and companies.

  • November 2022

    It all started here

    Started by Vishnu Avanthsa and Brandon Young, with 13 years of combined robotics expirence, and inspired by their coach Mr. Chu created B.R.A.C.E.

  • January 2023

    The idea becomes a reality

    The club registered with OSU and has huge hopes to help people locally and beyond.
